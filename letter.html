Dear you,

I still miss the way your name used to arrive before anything else did.

Some feelings do not leave; they just hold their breath inside ordinary hours, waiting for the smallest thing to bring them back.

I keep thinking love would be easier if it did not love so deeply, if it did not return in light, in music, in empty chairs, in the silence after a sentence ends.

Maybe that is why I still long for what is already gone, and why every room remembers more than I do.

Even now, I do not know whether I am letting go or simply learning how to carry absence more gently.
